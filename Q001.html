Problem Statement:

There is one spiritual center which wants to manage their devotee’s data. Devotees pays the donations every month. They are looking for the system through which they manage this information via web. Help them to achieve the same. They need to have the report how much donation they are getting every month.

There are two types of login. Admin and devotees. Admin can add, edit and display the details of devotees and their payment. Devotees can pay online their payment via sending an an otp to their registered email. 

Help them to achieve the same.

Task 1: Design The database of the same.

Task 2: Make the API. 

Task 3: Design the Front End for the user.
